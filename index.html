Web Basics with PHP Training

This is an entry-level training program that will take you through an introduction of web development with php.

Each participating student is assigned a block of training material pages to convert for web as we cover the topics in each chapter.... html, css, javascript, php, etc.

This has the ulterior motive of getting my training materials online, so it is a tremendous help to me! At the same time, I am here for one-on-one questions, and the ultimate goal of this training is to provide entry level people with the knowledge needed to take on some paying project work.

So far everyone who is doing the training is very part time, covering about one chapter per week.

Below you will find the chapter outline grouped into sections. There are some other advanced topics I'd like to cover, so the outline will change and grow as I assemble additional information.

For students who do not wish to particpate hands-on, please feel free to ask questions on the content or to embellish it. All questions pertaining to the training program may be sent to: training@infsoln.com

How to participate in web training

Step one is to learn the material in Section 1, which begins html training.

When you get to Chapter 6, HTML in practice, you can begin participating in the training program. Follow the instructions in that chapter to sign-up and to receive your assignments.

As you progress through the information in each chapter, you will apply the principles to the chapters in your assigned section.

Additionally, every student is asked to provide feedback on content, perform code reviews, and create quiz questions for other students' sections.

Good luck, and I look forward to hearing from you!
